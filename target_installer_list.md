## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: LinkedIn Outreach (Week 1)
- Target Tier 1 companies (PSW Energy, G8 Energy, Nuvolt)
- Personalized connection requests to decision makers
- Follow up with value proposition message

### Phase 2: Email Campaign (Week 1-2)
- Send personalized emails to Tier 2 & 3 companies
- A/B test subject lines and messaging
- Include "First Lead FREE" offer

### Phase 3: Follow-up Sequence (Week 2-3)
- 3-day follow-up for non-responders
- 7-day follow-up with case study
- 14-day final follow-up with urgency

### Phase 4: Phone Outreach (Week 3-4)
- Call companies with phone numbers listed
- Warm introduction referencing email
- Book demo calls

## Target Metrics
- **Goal**: 50-100 installer contacts in database
- **Expected Response Rate**: 10-15% (5-15 responses)
- **Expected Conversion**: 20-30% of responders (1-5 signed installers)
- **Timeline**: 4 weeks to first paying customer
